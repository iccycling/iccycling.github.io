Road & Indoor Cycling

Whether you’re hitting the tarmac or spinning indoors, join our road and indoor cycling community for all levels.

Road Cycling

Join our vibrant Road Cycling group for weekly rides in the beautiful Richmond Park and surrounding areas. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned rider, our rides offer the perfect blend of challenge, community, and fun.

We meet every Wednesday afternoon for beginner-friendly laps and every Sunday for a longer ride — usually 50+ km at a steady pace of around 28–30 km/h. The routes vary but often include scenic rides out to Windsor or hillier terrain in Surrey, with a well-deserved long coffee break midway.

Please bring your helmet, enough food and water for the ride, and a puncture repair kit. A road bike is required to join the rides. Membership is essential as it provides access to subsidised club kit and opportunities like a summer trip abroad.

Indoor Cycling

We know cycling in London comes with its challenges — busy roads, limited storage for road bikes, unpredictable weather, and busy schedules. That’s why we also support indoor cycling as a flexible and effective way to keep fit.

Whether you train on a smart trainer at home or use the Wattbikes at Ethos Gym on campus, our club is committed to expanding its indoor cycling offerings. Indoor cycling lets you train regardless of weather or daylight, provides consistent conditions, and fits easily into even the busiest days.

It’s also ideal for beginners without a road bike who want to build a solid endurance base. Tip: Use the Wattbikes at Ethos Gym and install the Wattbike app to track your performance — and share your rides with us in our Strava group!

Alumni no longer living in London are warmly invited to join our indoor community as well. We hope to grow our indoor program further and welcome your ideas and support. Feel free to reach out with suggestions — or better yet, get involved and help us develop new offerings!
