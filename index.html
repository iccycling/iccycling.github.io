About the Club

Imperial Cycling Club is a student-run community for all cycling levels and disciplines. We ride, train, race – and most importantly, we support each other and have fun on and off the bike. Based in London and backed by the Student Union, we welcome everyone: from complete beginners to seasoned racers.

Learn more

Road & Indoor Biking

Join us for weekly road rides through London and beyond – whether you're new to road cycling or training for BUCS races. We ride together, support each other, and make it fun for all levels. Prefer to train indoors? You can still connect with us through Zwift, Strava, or on gym bikes like the Wattbike – and be part of the club wherever you ride.

Learn more

MTB & Adventure

From skills sessions to full-day rides at Swinley or BikePark Wales – ICCC MTB welcomes all levels to experience the thrill of mountain biking in a friendly, supportive group.
