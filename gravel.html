Gravel & Tour

Explore gravel routes, scenic tours, and bikepacking adventures with fellow cycling enthusiasts.

Gravel Biking

Join our Gravel Biking community to explore mixed surfaces, quiet country roads, and hidden trails. Whether you’re new to gravel or an experienced rider, it’s a great way to discover new routes and enjoy the outdoors.

The club encourages members to organise group rides and share their favourite paths. Bring your gravel bike, enjoy the ride, and connect with others who love adventure off the main roads.

Even without official events yet, membership gives you the chance to meet fellow enthusiasts and start building your own rides and mini-tours.

Membership offers the chance to meet fellow gravel enthusiasts, exchange ideas for gear and routes, and participate in club-organised events — even if we don't yet run official rides regularly.

Tour & Bikepacking

Discover the joy of multi-day rides and self-supported adventures with our Tour & Bikepacking community. Whether you’re exploring nearby regions or tackling longer journeys, this is for riders who love freedom, flexibility, and adventure on two wheels.

Plan your routes, pack your essentials, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow enthusiasts. Membership gives you access to tips, route ideas, and a community eager to share experiences and inspiration for your next ride.
