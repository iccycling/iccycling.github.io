Urban & More

Commute. Culture. Gravel. Track. Bikepacking. If it rolls and doesn’t fit the mould, this is your place.

Urban Cycling

For many Imperial members, cycling is part of everyday life in London. Despite challenges like heavy traffic and limited bike storage, cycling remains an important part of the urban lifestyle.

The club aims to better support urban cycling and commuting in the future, even though there are no concrete offers yet. We welcome anyone interested in urban cycling to get involved and help develop new ideas.

This way, the club can become more appealing to those who see cycling as part of their daily city life or lifestyle.

More

Other cycling disciplines: Bikepacking, gravel riding, track cycling, and other trendy disciplines are currently less represented in the club. The club is still small and can’t offer dedicated programs yet, but if you’re interested, please get in touch — maybe you’ll find like-minded riders!

FAQ Urban & Other

Yes! We welcome riders with all types of bikes, including gravel, cyclocross, and other alternative styles.

The club supports commuting, urban cycling as a lifestyle, and is open to emerging disciplines like bikepacking and gravel riding.

Currently, group rides for urban and commuting cyclists are informal, but the club aims to develop more activities in this area soon.

Workshops are planned for the future. Meanwhile, members are encouraged to share knowledge and ideas to help build these offerings.

The club currently does not offer bike rental or sharing options. For commuting-related services, please check the Imperial website for available resources.

Get involved by joining club events, following our social media channels, or contacting us directly to find like-minded urban riders.
