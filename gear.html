Heritage ICL Racing Kit★ NEW

To mark the launch of the website, we’ve created a Heritage Jersey – a first design inspired by brands like MAAP, Rapha and Pas Normal Studios that combine urban style with cycling performance. The jerseys were designed using Sportful’s online configurator. Thanks to sublimation printing, custom designs are available from just one piece. As an outfitter for WorldTour teams, Sportful stands for top-tier quality at accessible prices. If you’re interested in getting a jersey, we’d love to hear from you. Feel free to take inspiration from the brands mentioned above – we’re open to ideas and collaboration on future designs. Prices depend on membership status, order quantity, and manufacturer offers. Jerseys start from around £60.
