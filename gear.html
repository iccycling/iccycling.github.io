Club Wear & Gear

Ride in style – explore our kits, team wear, and more

New Club Kit

We have developed a new jersey design as a draft proposal. It has yet to be discussed by the club. It is inspired by brands like MAAP, Rapha, Pas Normal Studios etc. that combine urban style with cycling performance. The jerseys were created using Sportful's online configurator. Thanks to sublimation printing, customised designs are available from just one piece. As an outfitter for World Tour teams, Sportful is known for the highest quality while offering affordable prices. If you are interested in a jersey, we look forward to hearing from you. You are welcome to get inspired by the brands mentioned above and we can work together on a new design for a club jersey. Prices depend on membership status, quantity ordered and offers from manufacturers. Prices start from around 60 GBP for jerseys. We hope to be able to offer more club wear such as hoodies and cycling gear in the future.
