Club Wear & Gear

Ride in style – explore our kits, team wear, and more

ICCC Club Wear

From high-quality racing kits to hoodies and casual wear, ICCC blends the performance edge of road cycling brands like MAAP or Pas Normal Studios with the laid-back vibe of a college club. Our range is designed so you look and feel great — not just in competition or on group rides, but also on the way to lectures through Hyde Park and South Kensington. We offer more than just full race kits, and we’re always open to ideas for new club wear. If you’re interested, reach out — we’re happy to share tips on where to find quality cycling gear at a fair price, whether it’s new or second-hand. Check our blog for a beginner’s guide to saving on cycling equipment, or explore affordable gems on platforms like Vinted for top-notch pre-loved kit.

Heritage ICL Racing Kit★ NEW

To mark the launch of the website, we’ve created a Heritage Jersey – a first design inspired by brands like MAAP, Rapha and Pas Normal Studios that combine urban style with cycling performance. The jerseys were designed using Sportful’s online configurator. Thanks to sublimation printing, custom designs are available from just one piece. As an outfitter for WorldTour teams, Sportful stands for top-tier quality at accessible prices. If you’re interested in getting a jersey, we’d love to hear from you. Feel free to take inspiration from the brands mentioned above – we’re open to ideas and collaboration on future designs. Prices depend on membership status, order quantity, and manufacturer offers. Jerseys start from around £60.
