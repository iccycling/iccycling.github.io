The Club

Racing, exploring, commuting or just rolling – Imperial College Cycling Club welcomes all riders. Join us and be part of a growing, open, and diverse cycling community.

Who We Are

ICCC focuses on road cycling and mountain biking, with regular group rides and weekend trips (see Road/MTB pages). We also take part in BUCS events and local races. But we're more than just a racing club.

We welcome alumni, commuters, indoor cyclists and everyone for whom cycling is part of their lifestyle. Whether you're into bikepacking, track, or just getting around town – you're invited to be part of our community.

What We Do

  • Weekly Road Group Rides – for all levels, from steady to fast
  • Regular MTB Trail Rides – plus weekend trips to trail centres
  • BUCS races & local events – open to all interested riders
  • Strava Club – connect, share and motivate each other
  • Discounted Team Wear – race kit, hoodies and more

Cycling involves certain risks, as you’re exposed to traffic without a protective shell and often travel at speed. Accidents, sometimes serious, can happen, so it’s important to ensure you have the right insurance coverage. Check what is provided by the NHS and Imperial, and consider whether additional insurance is advisable, especially when cycling abroad.

Wearing a helmet is mandatory for all club activities. For mountain biking, extra protective gear like pads is strongly recommended (see MTB FAQ). When riding in low light, make sure your bike has effective lights, reflectors, and that you wear high-visibility clothing. Always carry a charged phone in case you need to call for help.

Regularly maintain your bike, focusing on brakes and carbon forks. If you’re not confident doing this yourself, visit a bike shop for professional servicing. Taking these precautions helps keep your rides safe and enjoyable.
